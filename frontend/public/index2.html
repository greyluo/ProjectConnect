About Us

This project started from LA Hackathon in 2023.

  • Interest-based project matching
  • Project creation and collaboration
  • Progress tracking and milestones
  • Networking and community building

Through ProjectConnect, students can connect with peers, faculty members, and industry professionals involved in projects, fostering a sense of community and creating valuable networking opportunities. The platform's project management feature enables users to set milestones, track progress, and collaborate on tasks, allowing them to gain a sense of accomplishment as they complete milestones and contribute to the project's success.
